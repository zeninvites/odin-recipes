Pasta Salad

Description

This pasta salad is nice and colorful, easy to make, and great for summer picnics or parties.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning Mix
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
